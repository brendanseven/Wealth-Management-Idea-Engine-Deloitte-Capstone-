# Data Access Notes

## Competitor Product Launch Tracker (`competitor_tracker_merged.csv`)

This dataset was manually compiled by the team from publicly available sources. No proprietary or confidential data is included. All information was sourced from:

- **PitchBook** (accessed via Carnegie Mellon University institutional license): private market fund launches, fund sizes, strategy types
- **SEC EDGAR** (public): new fund registration filings (N-2, N-1A forms)
- **Firm press releases and newsroom pages** (public): product launch announcements
- **Industry publications** (public): InvestmentNews, Barron's, Citywire, Financial Planning

Coverage: 49 product launches across 10 firms, September 2024 through March 2026.

## Macroeconomic Data

All macroeconomic indicators are sourced from **FRED** (Federal Reserve Economic Data), a free public database maintained by the Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis. No access restrictions.

Series used: FEDFUNDS, DGS10, CPIAUCSL, A191RL1Q225SBEA, UMCSENT, UNRATE.

## Fund Flow Data

Fund flow data is sourced from **ICI** (Investment Company Institute) public statistical reports and **Morningstar** public fund flow summaries. No access restrictions on the data used.

## Faculty-Provided Consumer Dataset

A consumer demographic dataset (3,000 records across three wealth tiers: Mass Market, Mass Affluent, Affluent Plus) was provided by the faculty advisor. This dataset is **not included in the repository** as it was provided for educational use only. It contains no personally identifiable information. The dataset was assessed for potential use in a client segmentation layer but was not incorporated into the final prototype.

## Sponsor Data

No proprietary Deloitte data was used in this project. All data sources are publicly available or faculty-provided for educational purposes.
